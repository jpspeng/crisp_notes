# CRISP survey data documentation 

* `record_id`: Unique REDCap survey record number.
* `age_group`: Age category (`0` = 33 years or younger; `1` = older than 33 years).
* `sex`: Sex (`1` = Woman; `2` = Man; `3` = Non-binary; `4` = Prefer not to say).
* `pet_preference`: Pet preference (`1` = Dogs; `2` = Cats; `3` = Both dogs and cats; `4` = Neither).
* `morning_or_evening_person`: Time-of-day preference (`1` = Morning; `2` = Evening; `3` = Equal or neither).
* `food_preference`: Food preference (`1` = Gourmet; `2` = Upscale; `3` = Home cooking; `4` = Fast food).
* `spent_time_in_nature`: Spent at least two hours in nature during the past week (`0` = No; `1` = Yes).
* `interacted_with_clubs`: Interacted with at least one club or organization during the past week (`0` = No; `1` = Yes).
* `resistance_training_workouts`: Number of resistance-training workouts performed during the past week (`0` = 0 or 1; `2`–`9` = corresponding number; `10` = 10 or more).
* `hours_of_sleep`: Average number of hours slept per night (`0` = 0 or 1; `2`–`9` = corresponding number; `10` = 10 or more).
* `packaged_snacks_per_day`: Average number of packaged snacks consumed per day (`0` = 0 or 1; `2`–`9` = corresponding number; `10` = 10 or more).
* `hours_sitting`: Average number of hours spent sitting per day (`0` = 0 or 1; `2`–`9` = corresponding number; `10` = 10 or more).
* `servings_vegetable`: Average number of vegetable servings consumed per day (`0` = 0 or 1; `2`–`9` = corresponding number; `10` = 10 or more).
* `cardio_exercise`: Total number of minutes of cardiorespiratory exercise performed during the past week. The original categories were converted to minutes as follows: less than 30 minutes = 15, 30 minutes = 30, 45 minutes = 45, 1 hour = 60, 1.5 hours = 90, 2 hours = 120, 2.5 hours = 150, 3 hours = 180, 3.5 hours = 210, 4 hours = 240, 4.5 hours = 270, and 5 or more hours = 300.
* `anxious_depressed_or_irritable`: Frequency of being bothered by emotional problems such as feeling anxious, depressed, or irritable during the past week (`1` = Never; `2` = Rarely; `3` = Sometimes; `4` = Often; `5` = Always).
* `fatigue_rating`: Average fatigue rating (`1` = None; `2` = Mild; `3` = Moderate; `4` = Severe; `5` = Very severe).
